Class Systems

Rogue

The rogue is a lone warrior who lives and travels in the shadows. A dagger will be your only (and best) friend, and your enemies will fall to your knife at close proximity. Your weakness is to engage in face-to-face combat; your goal is to avoid the enemy until you are at their back. With your footsteps as silent as the night, your wits as sharp as your blade, and a face that no one (alive) knows, they will never se you coming, and they won't have the time to know what hit them.

Warrior

Warriors lives and dies defending the backs of his/her brothers and sisters. The sword is their ally, his soul, and his life. His brothers are the reason for that sword, and for all else. The warrior faces his enemy face-to-face in battle, garbed in his choice and weight of armor.
